FAQ

Questions you might have

What does the free analysis check? +
We scan your website and check your presence across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews for your core keywords. You'll see your AI Visibility Score, which competitors are getting cited instead of you, and exactly where the gaps are. Takes about 30 seconds.
Do I need developers to set this up? +
Zero. Connect WordPress with your existing credentials or GitHub with a personal access token. The setup wizard takes under 10 minutes and walks you through everything.
What's the difference between SEO and AI Visibility? +
SEO is about ranking on Google. AI Visibility is about being cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini and Google AI Overviews when people ask questions. More people now ask AI than search Google. Different channel, different metrics, bigger opportunity. We handle both.
Which industries do you support? +
Auto body, automotive dealerships, dental, legal, medical, real estate, SaaS, e-commerce, restaurants, education, and more. The AI adapts keyword strategy and content templates to your vertical automatically.
How is the AI Visibility Score calculated? +
We query ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Overviews with your target keywords. For each query, we check if your business is cited, partially cited, or not cited — and compare against your top 3 competitors. The score (0-100) is weighted by query volume and search intent. Updated weekly.
