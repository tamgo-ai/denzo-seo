Common questions

Do I need to bring my own Google credentials?

No. Denzo uses its own Google Cloud project for OAuth — clients just click "Connect Google Business Profile" or "Connect Search Console" inside their dashboard and authorize their own account. Tokens are stored encrypted per-tenant. Each client's data stays isolated from every other client's.

What happens after I subscribe?

You're redirected to your dashboard, the agent pipeline becomes available for the number of clients your plan allows, and Mission Control opens. The first run typically generates 100–500 pages within an hour, all of which you can preview, edit, or reject before publishing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There's no contract. Click "Manage billing" inside the app and cancel — Stripe will keep your subscription active until the end of the current billing period and then stop charging.

Do I keep my generated content if I cancel?

Yes. Anything Denzo published to your GitHub repo or WordPress site is yours — it lives on your infrastructure, not ours. The Denzo dashboard goes back to read-only on the free tier.

What if my vertical isn't supported?

The "general" vertical is a safe fallback that still produces strong content. We add new verticals on request — most take a day or two to ship as a fully-tuned page matrix. Email us with what you do.
