FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from hiring an SEO agency?
Agencies charge $3,000–$8,000/month, take 60–90 days to onboard, and deliver inconsistent results. DENZO SEO runs its entire 10-agent pipeline in under 20 minutes, generates pages at machine scale, and costs a fraction of the price. The key difference: we go programmatic — generating hundreds of precisely targeted pages, not one blog post a week.
Will Google penalize me for programmatically generated pages?
Not when done right. Google penalizes thin, duplicate "doorway pages." DENZO SEO generates unique, contextually rich pages for each location/service/brand combination — each one passes Content Optimizer and E-E-A-T review before publishing. Every client we have has seen ranking increases, not penalties.
What industries does DENZO SEO work for?
Any service-area or multi-location business. Top verticals: automotive (collision repair, dealerships), insurance, real estate, medical/dental, legal, home services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing), and restaurants. If your business serves multiple cities or has multiple product/service lines, programmatic SEO will work for you.
How long before I see results?
Most clients see Google impressions climbing within 2–3 weeks of publishing. Meaningful ranking improvements and traffic typically appear in 4–8 weeks. Results depend on domain age, competition level, and number of pages published. We show you the data in real time via the Rank Tracker dashboard.
Do I need technical skills to use DENZO SEO?
No. You connect your website via our WordPress plugin or GitHub integration, fill in a brief about your business (locations, services, competitors), and hit Run. The agents handle everything else. Setup takes under 10 minutes. Your onboarding call walks you through every step.
Does DENZO SEO also work for AI search (ChatGPT, Perplexity)?
Yes — this is a core differentiator. Our E-E-A-T Architect and Schema Engineer explicitly optimize every page to be cited by LLM-based search engines. We structure content as entities, not just keywords. 67% of people now start searches in AI tools — we make sure your business is the answer they get.
